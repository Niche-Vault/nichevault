## 1. Industrial Valve Manufacturers UK

**Niche:** Industrial Valve Manufacturers UK
**SIC codes:**
- **28120** — Manufacture of taps and valves (primary)
- **28291** — Manufacture of industrial process equipment not elsewhere classified (secondary — some valve system integrators)
**Company count estimate:** 1,200+ active companies on Companies House under SIC 28120 alone (covering manufacturers of industrial valves, taps, pneumatic/hydraulic valve systems, and valve servicing)
**Target job titles:**
- Managing Director / Owner
- Sales Director / Business Development Manager
- Procurement / Supply Chain Manager
- Technical Director / Engineering Manager
**Data availability:** ✅ Excellent. SIC 28120 is precise. Companies House has full registered address, incorporation date, company type, and filing records. Easy to filter out non-trading/dormant.
**Why this niche:** Industrial valves are mission-critical components in oil & gas, water treatment, chemical processing, and manufacturing. Sales teams targeting these plants need to reach valve suppliers for procurement partnerships, distribution deals, or servicing contracts. A £75 list gives instant access to a hard-to-crawl B2B segment not available from free directories.
**Verdict:** ✅ PASS — >200 companies, specific SIC, easy to validate, high B2B value.

## 2. UK Courier & Delivery Services

**Niche:** UK Courier & Delivery Services
**SIC codes:**
- **53201** — Licensed carriers (licensed as a common carrier)
- **53202** — Unlicensed carriers (courier services not licensed as a common carrier)
- **49410** — Freight transport by road (secondary — larger logistics firms)
**Company count estimate:** 8,000+ active companies (combined SIC 53201 + 53202 — this is a massive sector with many local/regional couriers)
**Target job titles:**
- Owner / Managing Director
- Operations Manager / Logistics Manager
- Fleet Manager
- Sales & Marketing Manager
**Data availability:** ✅ Excellent. Both 53201 and 53202 are specific courier codes. Easy to distinguish between national carriers (49410) and local courier services (53201/53202). Many sole traders and Ltd companies.
**Why this niche:** Thousands of independent courier companies across the UK are hungry for clients — e-commerce brands, retailers, wholesalers all need delivery partners. A verified list lets a salesperson target these businesses for fleet financing, insurance, vehicle leasing, tracking software, or packaging supplies. Huge addressable market with clear pain points.
**Verdict:** ✅ PASS — huge company count, clean SIC codes, strong buyer intent.

## 3. UK Security Companies (Alarm/CCTV Installers)

**Niche:** UK Security Companies (Alarm & CCTV Installers)
**SIC codes:**
- **80200** — Security systems service activities (primary — alarm monitoring, CCTV installation, access control)
- **80100** — Private security activities (secondary — manned guarding, may overlap but less relevant)
**Company count estimate:** 4,000+ active companies under SIC 80200 (this covers the bulk of alarm installers, CCTV integrators, and security system service companies)
**Target job titles:**
- Managing Director / Owner
- Operations Director
- Technical Director / Installation Manager
- Sales Manager / Business Development Manager
**Data availability:** ✅ Excellent. SIC 80200 is very specific to security systems services. Companies House data cleanly captures the target market. SIC 80100 should be excluded (manned guarding is a different buyer).
**Why this niche:** Security installers are a classic B2B target for equipment suppliers (cameras, alarms, access control hardware), software vendors (remote monitoring platforms, CRM), insurance brokers (liability coverage), and vehicle leasing (fleet of vans). They are easy to cold-call and often run by owner-operators who make buying decisions directly.
**Verdict:** ✅ PASS — strong company count, precise SIC, excellent B2B targeting.

## 4. UK Landscaping & Grounds Maintenance

**Niche:** UK Landscaping & Grounds Maintenance
**SIC codes:**
- **81300** — Landscape service activities (primary — gardening, landscaping, grounds maintenance)
- **01610** — Support activities for crop production (secondary — agricultural maintenance, mostly exclude)
**Company count estimate:** 12,000+ active companies under SIC 81300 (this is a very large sector thanks to low barriers to entry and many Ltd companies in domestic/commercial landscaping)
**Target job titles:**
- Director / Owner
- Operations Manager

**Data availability:** ✅ Good. SIC 81300 is a single broad code that covers everything from one-man gardening to large commercial grounds maintenance firms. Some manual filtering needed to separate domestic gardeners from commercial landscaping firms (e.g., by looking for keywords like "commercial," "contract," "estate," or filtering by employee count).
**Why this niche:** Commercial landscaping companies need equipment (mowers, tools, vehicles), software (scheduling, CRM, quoting), insurance, and staff uniforms. A verified list filtered to Ltd companies with turnover indicators targets the serious players rather than cash-in-hand gardeners. Great for equipment dealers, software vendors, and insurance brokers.
**Verdict:** ✅ PASS — very large market, valid SIC code, some filtering required but viable.

## 5. UK Caterers & Event Catering

**Niche:** UK Caterers & Event Catering
**SIC codes:**
- **56210** — Event catering activities (primary — wedding caterers, corporate event catering, outside caterers)
- **56290** — Other food service activities (secondary — includes mobile food stands, some contract catering)
- **56100** — Restaurants and mobile food service (EXCLUDE — this is the restaurants niche already done)
**Company count estimate:** 5,000+ active companies under SIC 56210 (dedicated event caterers and outside caterers)
**Target job titles:**
- Managing Director / Owner
- Head Chef / Operations Director
- Events Manager / Sales Manager
- Catering Manager
**Data availability:** ✅ Good. SIC 56210 is quite specific — it excludes restaurants (56100) and industrial catering (56290). Some overlap with 56290 exists; best to focus on 56210 only for the cleanest list.
**Why this niche:** Event caterers are a prime target for commercial kitchen equipment suppliers, food distributors, event venue partnerships, staffing agencies (temporary hospitality staff), and marketing services (wedding fair presence, website design). They have clear B2B needs and are easy to find decision-makers for.
**Verdict:** ✅ PASS — substantial company count, precise SIC, strong B2B use cases.

## 6. UK Property Developers (Residential)

**Niche:** UK Property Developers (Residential)
**SIC codes:**
- **41100** — Development of building projects (primary — this is the main SIC for property developers who buy land, obtain planning, build/refurbish, and sell)
- **68100** — Buying and selling of own real estate (secondary — some developers use this, but overlaps with estate agents)
**Company count estimate:** 15,000+ active companies under SIC 41100 (this is a massive category covering everything from small developers doing a single conversion to large national housebuilders)
**Target job titles:**
- Managing Director / Founder
- Development Director / Project Manager
- Land & Planning Manager / Acquisitions Director
- Commercial Director
**Data availability:** ✅ Excellent. SIC 41100 is the perfect code. Companies House captures developers of all sizes. Can be further refined by company age (newer = active developments), filing data (turnover thresholds), and region.
**Why this niche:** Property developers are high-value B2B targets — they need architects, structural engineers, solicitors, surveyors, construction materials, project financing, insurance, and marketing services. A developer list is arguably one of the most versatile B2B lead lists available because so many service businesses want to reach them.
**Verdict:** ✅ PASS — very large market, ideal SIC, extremely high B2B demand.

## 7. UK Office Furniture Suppliers

**Niche:** UK Office Furniture Suppliers
**SIC codes:**
- **31090** — Manufacture of other furniture (includes office furniture manufacturing)
- **47599** — Retail sale of furniture, lighting, and other household articles (includes office furniture retail)
- **46650** — Wholesale of office furniture (secondary — B2B wholesale distributors)
**Company count estimate:** 1,500+ active companies (combined across 31090 + 46650 when filtered for office furniture — best to target SIC 46650 [wholesale] and keyword filter 31090 for "office")
**Target job titles:**
- Managing Director / Owner
- Sales Director / Showroom Manager
- Procurement Manager
- Operations / Logistics Manager
**Data availability:** ✅ Good. No single perfect SIC for "office furniture only," but SIC 46650 (wholesale of office furniture) is very accurate. SIC 31092 specifically covers "manufacture of office and shop furniture." A refined approach using SIC 46650 + 31092 yields a clean list.
**Why this niche:** Office furniture suppliers are in-demand as companies return to offices and refit workspaces. They need commercial vehicle leasing (delivery vans), warehouse space, wood/metal materials supply, software (ERP, inventory management), and trade show services. A tight, 100-company verified list of actual office furniture specialists (not general furniture retailers) is valuable.
**Verdict:** ✅ PASS — moderate but sufficient company count, multiple relevant SIC codes, clear B2B angle.

This is a growing sector driven by ESG regulations and landfill taxes. Decision-makers are easy to reach and the industry is relationship-driven.
**Verdict:** ✅ PASS — solid company count, clear SIC codes, strong B2B ecosystem.

## 9. UK Printing & Signage Companies

**Niche:** UK Printing & Signage Companies
**SIC codes:**
- **18120** — Other printing (not elsewhere classified) (primary — general commercial printers)
- **18110** — Printing of newspapers (secondary — likely exclude)
- **18129** — Other printing (this is actually the same as 18120; the full code)
- **47789** — Other retail sale of new goods in specialised stores (use for sign-makers — keyword filter needed)
- **32990** — Other manufacturing not elsewhere classified (some signage manufacturers)
**Company count estimate:** 5,000+ active companies under SIC 18120 (commercial printers) plus approx 1,500 signage-specific companies (identified by keyword filtering on Companies House)
**Target job titles:**
- Managing Director / Owner
- Sales Director / Account Manager
- Production Manager / Print Manager
- Operations Director
**Data availability:** ✅ Good. SIC 18120 is the primary code for commercial printers. Signage companies are harder to isolate by SIC alone — best approach is SIC 18120 + keyword filter ("sign," "signage," "banner," "display") or SIC 32990 + keyword. A combined printer+signage list is commercially viable.
**Why this niche:** Printers and sign-makers buy expensive capital equipment (digital presses, cutters, laminators), vast quantities of substrates, ink, vinyl, and software (DesignSoft, MIS systems). Vehicle wrap installers need commercial vans. This is a mature industry with clear seasonal buying patterns and well-attended trade shows, making it easy to research and sell to.
**Verdict:** ✅ PASS — strong company count, good SIC options, established B2B market.

## 10. UK Boat & Marine Repair Services

**Niche:** UK Boat & Marine Repair Services
**SIC codes:**
- **33150** — Repair and maintenance of ships and boats (primary — the perfect SIC for marine repair, boatyards, and marine engineering workshops)
- **30110** — Building of ships and floating structures (secondary — shipbuilding, exclude unless doing repair work)
- **30120** — Building of pleasure and sporting boats (secondary — boat builders who also do repair)
**Company count estimate:** 800+ active companies under SIC 33150 specifically (this is a specialist niche — smaller than others but still well over the 200 threshold)
**Target job titles:**
- Managing Director / Owner / Yard Manager
- Service Manager / Workshop Manager
- Marine Engineer / Technical Director
- Parts & Logistics Manager
**Data availability:** ✅ Excellent. SIC 33150 is one of the most precise SIC codes in the UK register — it cleanly captures marine/boat repair businesses. Very little noise.
**Why this niche:** Marine repair businesses need specialist tools, marine parts & equipment (engines, electronics, paints, fiberglass), slipway/hoist servicing, insurance, and software (job management, stock control). This is a high-ticket, relationship-driven industry where the businesses have clear needs and the owners are easy to identify. Many are coastal/rural and underserved by generic sales outreach. A verified list saves enormous research effort.
**Verdict:** ✅ PASS — meets >200 threshold with a very precise SIC, ideal for targeted B2B sales.
