Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

NicheVault sells verified UK business data sourced from Companies House, the UK's official public government registry. We do not collect, process, or sell personal consumer data.

Who we are

NicheVault is operated by Frederick Levy, based in Mandeville, Jamaica. We provide verified UK B2B lead lists sourced from Companies House and sold via Gumroad.

Contact: fred@nichevault.net

What data we collect about you

When you visit nichevault.net, we collect limited usage data via Plausible Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics tool. Plausible does not use cookies and does not collect personal identifiers. No data is shared with advertising networks.

When you make a purchase, your transaction is handled by Gumroad. We receive your email address and order details from Gumroad for the purpose of fulfilling your order. We do not store payment card information.

What data we sell

Our products contain UK company information sourced directly from Companies House via its public API. This data is publicly available and published by the UK government for transparency purposes. It includes:

  • Company name and registration number
  • Registered address
  • Company status and incorporation date
  • SIC codes (industry classification)
  • Director name and role, where part of the public Companies House record

We do not provide personal email addresses, personal phone numbers, or any data beyond what is publicly available in the Companies House registry.

Director data and UK GDPR

Director names included in our data are drawn from the public Companies House register, where they are published in the directors' capacity as company officers — not as private individuals. This is publicly available information under UK law.

Buyers are responsible for ensuring their own use of this data complies with applicable data protection and marketing laws, including UK GDPR and PECR, when using it for outreach or marketing purposes.

Data retention

We retain order records for accounting and legal compliance purposes. You may request deletion of your personal data (name, email) by contacting us at fred@nichevault.net.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact fred@nichevault.net.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised.
